Team registration

It MIGHT be free but don't count on it (we're trying our best to bring the price down, god bless the business team)

There are a few steps that are needed to register a student on our team. This page will take you through each step. A general Process overview can be found here. Theses forms should be filled out with a parent or guardian and student together.

  1. The Team Handbook

    Read the Team Handbook (the whole thing) which can be found here or on the right-hand side of the 1510 website. After doing so, you should be fine moving to the next step. If you ever have a question during the build season or competition, do not hesitate to look it up in the handbook.

  2. Register for FIRST STIMS

    Standing for: Student Team Information Member System. This is for the global competition that we compete in, instructions are provided on the site found here. It will ask you several questions, when it ask what program you are a part of answer: FRC and when it asks what team answer: 1510 The Wildcats. After you finish this section, it will send you and the coaches an email saying that you have completed this part of the registration.

    If you have any questions this document should answer them. If any questions remain, talk to the coach or a mentor. If you STILL have a question, um, go read the bible or sth, the answer's always in there.

  3. Filling the damn forms

    Here's how you do it: CLICK ME BOI.

    After that, take a deep breathe, prepare a ton of coffee (recommended) and fill these out. Good luck :)

    • Participant Code of Conduct
    • Release of Liability & Hold Harmless
    • Form B-Transportation and Release
    • Form C-Private Auto Driver & Passenger Release
    • Form E-Student Contract - Overnight or Out of State
    • Form F-Health History Overnight

After all the forms have been filled out, there should be six forms filled out, and turned into the coach or head mentor.

For students in need of financial aid, fill out this form and talk with the coach.
